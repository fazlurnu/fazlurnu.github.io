Muhammad Fazlur Rahman

I am a PhD candidate in Unmanned Traffic Management at TU Delft, the Netherlands 🇳🇱. My research interests include UAS, ATM/UTM, conflict detection and resolution, separation management, and aviation data.

Previously, I worked at Akkodis 🇫🇷 as a flight control law engineer and at ACSL 🇯🇵 as a drone engineer. I also completed internships at Airbus 🇫🇷 and Indonesian Aerospace 🇮🇩.

I earned my Master's degree in Aerospace Systems and Control from ISAE-SUPAERO 🇫🇷, and my Bachelor of Science in Aeronautics and Astronautics from Institut Teknologi Bandung 🇮🇩.
