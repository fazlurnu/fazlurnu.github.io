This is the first post on the blog. I've been meaning to keep a public notebook for
a while — somewhere to put the half-finished thoughts that don't fit into a paper,
a Twitter thread, or a GitHub README.

## What to expect

Three flavors of posts:

- **Memoir** — living and moving between countries.
- **Notes** — small things I learn about aviation data, drones, or just code.
- **Side quests** — chess, hobbies, food, the occasional rant.
